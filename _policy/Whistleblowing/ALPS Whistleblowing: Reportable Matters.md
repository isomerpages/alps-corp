<h2>ALPS Whistleblowing</h2>
<p><strong>Important:</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>DO NOT use this site to report incidents that presents an immediate threat to life or property. If you require emergency assistance, please contact your local authorities.</strong>
</p>
<hr>
<p>MOHH Group Whistleblowing Channel is an <strong>independent </strong>whistleblowing
service managed by an external provider, Deloitte &amp; Touche Financial
Advisory Pte Ltd.</p>
<p>Reports made through the MOHH Group Whistleblowing Channel are <strong>confidential</strong>.
Whistleblowers have the option to lodge a report anonymously. However,
whistleblowers are encouraged to identify themselves, so that further information
or clarification can be sought if required. Lack of information or clarity
may limit the ability to conduct a thorough investigation.</p>
<hr>
<p><strong>Reportable matters</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Asset Misappropriation / Falsification, Financial Statement Fraud, and/or
Fraud related activities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Corruption and Bribery</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Conflict of Interest</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Regulation Breach</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Safety Issues</p>
</li>
</ol>

<p>You do not need to know every detail before coming forward. Most reported
cases come to light through tips from well-meaning individuals who may
not have full knowledge of the facts. <strong>Reporting what you do know is sufficient and will be a meaningful first step.</strong>
</p>
<p>All whistleblowers who report in good faith will be afforded full protection
from any form of retaliation, and your identity will remain confidential.
It is important that the whistleblowing channel is used responsibly and
with integrity. Any misuse of this channel for malicious purposes or personal
gain is a serious breach that will be dealt with accordingly, including
potential disciplinary action.</p>
<p>We expect the exercise of sound judgement when using this channel.</p>
