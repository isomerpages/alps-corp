<p>The ALPS Green Procurement Guidelines align with SG2030 Green Procurement
Requirements under the GreenGovSG framework, which includes, but is not
limited to, Air-Conditioners, Refrigerators, Televisions, Lamps, Water
Fitting and Equipment, Building Products, Printing Paper, ICT Equipment
and Vehicles.</p>
<p>ALPS is committed to protecting the environment through its Procurement
and Supply Chain Practices. Since 2022, ALPS has gradually rolled out Green
Procurement across Public Healthcare Institutions to improve energy and
water efficiency, and to reduce and recycle waste.</p>
<p><strong>What's new:</strong>
</p>
<p>As a next step to support national sustainability goals and decarbonise
the healthcare supply chain, ALPS has launched a Sustainability Questionnaire
that will be used in our sourcing events.</p>
<p>Help us understand your current environmental practices, identify opportunities,
and make more sustainable procurement decisions.</p>
