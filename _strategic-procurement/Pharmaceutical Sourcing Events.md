<h4>TO GAIN DIRECT ACCESS INTO EVENTS</h4>
<ul>

under the column “Respond to Posting”.</p>
</li>

</li>
<li>
<p>Log in to <strong><a href="https://supplier.ariba.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">SAP Business Network</a></strong> by
entering your ID and password.</p>
