<h3>ALPS' STANDARD TERMS &amp; CONDITIONS</h3>
<p>All new and existing purchase orders will be governed by ALPS' <strong>Standard Terms &amp; Conditons</strong> and <strong>Master Agreements</strong>.</p>
<p>ALPS will retain the final sourcing decisions and approvals for contractual
commitments binding ALPS and vendors.</p>
<p>Please continue to supply the product(s) and/or service(s) in accordance
with existing contractual and business arrangements with ALPS.</p>
<p>Vendors are encouraged to refer to ALPS' contract template below to learn
more.</p>
